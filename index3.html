Research interests

Natural Language Processing (NLP) for Social Impact

Harnessing the power of language models to understand human communication has always fascinated me. I’m particularly driven to explore NLP's potential in education, healthcare, and social justice—how we can bridge gaps in accessibility, language barriers, and cultural divides using state-of-the-art models like GPT, BERT, and beyond.

Speech Recognition and Understanding

Speech is one of the most natural forms of communication, and enabling machines to comprehend it brings us closer to a seamless human-AI interaction. I'm passionate about developing speech models that are accurate, inclusive, and adaptive to diverse accents and languages, opening doors to next-gen assistive technologies and accessibility solutions.

AI for Healthcare Transformation

Leveraging ML to improve diagnostic accuracy, patient outcomes, and personalized medicine is a powerful frontier in healthcare. From predictive analytics to medical image interpretation and patient monitoring systems, I’m fascinated by the potential of AI to revolutionize how we approach patient care, making it more precise and accessible globally.

Sustainability and ML for a Greener Future

AI and sustainability make for an inspiring intersection. I'm deeply interested in exploring how machine learning can be applied to reduce environmental impacts—whether through optimizing resource usage in agriculture, energy efficiency, or understanding climate data. AI has the power to inform smarter choices for a more sustainable planet.
