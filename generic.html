Research Papers

Autonomous Systems

Improving the Performance of Autonomous Driving Through Deep Reinforcement Learning

Institution: SIT, Pune

Published In: Sustainability, MDPI

This paper discusses training an autonomous car navigation model using DQN, DDPG, and PPO algorithms. It highlights DQN's superior performance in short training times and the potential for significant improvement with ε-decay, while indicating the need for further exploration of PPO with extended training periods. Read the paper.

Natural Language Processing

Towards Efficient Knowledge Extraction: NLP-Based Summarization of Research Paper Introductions

Institution: SCAAI, India

Accepted For Publication In: IJAI

This research presents a hybrid model combining abstractive and extractive techniques for summarizing research paper introductions, achieving an average score of 5.8, nearly matching BERT (6.88) and outperforming T5 (4.8 and 5.4). (Accepted - To be published in the next edition).

Computer Vision and Deep Learning

Comparative Analysis Of Indian Sign Language Recognition Using Deep Learning Models

Institution: SIT, Pune

Published In: Forum for Linguistic Studies

This study involved building an Indian Sign Language (ISL) dataset from scratch and conducting comprehensive research comparing five deep learning models for ISL-to-text translation. It employed image processing techniques to evaluate the performance of Densenet, Inception, Resnet, VGG19, and VGG16 models. The optimum results were achieved with ResNet-50, attaining an accuracy of 98.25% and an F1-score of 99.34%. InceptionNet V3 was identified as the least favorable model with an accuracy of 66.75% and an F1-score of 70.89%. Read the paper.
